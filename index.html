Understanding Knee Pain in Ayurveda

Knee pain can result from aging, joint degeneration, inflammation, or lifestyle imbalances. Ayurveda views joint discomfort as a result of aggravated Vata dosha, which affects lubrication and flexibility in the joints. Instead of focusing only on temporary relief, many individuals explore Ayurvedic treatment for knee pain for a more holistic and long-term approach.

Why Kerala is Known for Ayurvedic Joint Care

Kerala has long been recognized as a center for authentic traditional healing practices. Many people travel specifically for knee pain treatment in Kerala due to its experienced practitioners and classical therapy methods.

Common Ayurvedic Therapies for Knee Support

  • Abhyanga: Medicated oil massage to support circulation.
  • Janu Basti: Warm oil therapy focused directly on the knee.
